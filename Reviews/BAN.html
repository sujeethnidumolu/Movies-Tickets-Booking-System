Here is my review for the movie BHARATH ANE NENU
4

STORY

Oxford University graduate and UK resident, Bharat returns to home country India, following a tragedy in his family.

ACTING

Mahesh delivers a superb performance that his fan have been anticipating for years

DIRECTION

In this film, director Koratala rightly played to Mahesh’s strengths by combining class elements with hero elevation

MUSIC

Hero elevation in the thirty minutes pre-interval and post interval scenes are excellent

SCREENPLAY

On flip side , with so many highly elevated scenes, post press meet the film “climax “ is a “wee bit” let down
